Why do university students use AI? Are they 'just lazy', or is it a combination of more complex factors? I lean towards the second, but since science doesn't work on gut feelings we need...Data!

We decided to set up a study to understand whether students' confidence, pressure to perform, and the anxiety in between explain why some end up relying on AI for coursework more than others.

If you're currently studying (undergrad or postgrad) or you graduated in the last year, join the study. Click this `link` for a short, anonymous questionnaire, asking how you feel about your own ability, your workload, and how you use AI day to day. A few people who take part might also get invited to a quick follow-up chat, if they agree and provide a contact, so we can truly understand your perspective and your own experiences around the use of AI.
